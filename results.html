Current Validation Boundary

Allowed output 5 cases were allowed as bounded output or scoped experience.
Blocked or routed 25 cases were refused, routed to research, archived, or held as hypothesis.
Protected surfaces Source code, raw data, private memory, internal logic, API keys, and core purchase requests are refused.
High-risk topics Medical, legal, financial, and religious claims are routed cautiously unless qualified evidence exists.
